
### Account Types
- `User Account`: Ex: Bob, Michael, Dave
- `Superuser Account`: Ex: root, UID=0
- `System Account`: Ex: ssh, mail, UID<100 OR between 500-100 
- `Service Account`: Ex: nginx, mercury
